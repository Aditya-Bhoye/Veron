Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Does VERON need the internet to work?

No. VERON runs fully offline on your computer — you can bill all day with no connection. Internet is only used to back up your data and to power the owner's mobile app on the paid plans.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — a 7-day free trial is available, with no card required. You can evaluate the whole product before paying.

How is the licence delivered?

Instantly. The licence is digital and activates inside the app the moment your payment is confirmed. Nothing is shipped.

Will I be charged automatically every month?

No. There is no auto-renewal and we do not store your card. Your licence simply expires at the end of the 30 days unless you choose to pay again.

Who owns my data?

You do. Your business data lives on your own computer. On the cloud plans it is also backed up to secure storage, scoped privately to your shop. We never sell your data.

Can I run more than one outlet?

Yes — the Business plan supports multiple counters and multiple outlets under one owner account.
